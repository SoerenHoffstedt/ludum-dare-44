- Your team is on a space ship that has gotten into trouble with a 
  facist warmonger. You have to reach your homeplanet to be safe.
- Your life depends on your ship. You need fuel to reach home
  but are out of money. Trade parts of your ship for fuel.
- The parts of your ship determine your abilities: Damage, Defense, Speed, and Scanning.
- Damage done in battles is calculated by: Your Damage - Enemy Defense.
  High Speed allows you to flee battles. More details on the battle screen.
- Speed determines how fast you travel between planets.
- The strength of your Scanning system determines how far ahead you can analyze planets.
  For each scanning point you can see four more tiles ahead.
